About Hamat

Due to the rapid development in industrial field and adding new equipment, the common use of technology, and its concomitant creation of new risks to workers and the workplace, it was necessary to highlight these risks and educate workers and employers about them. So we established Hamat training center to lead the field of training needs for our clients.
